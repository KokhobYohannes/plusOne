About Plus One

At Plus One, we believe that the best memories are created when we share it with others. This is why we wanted to have a platform for DMV locals to find dates or locals within their area to attend events with. Here, Plus One users can create events to celebrate their lives' milestones and events, big and small from a wine tasting, to a baby shower to a graduation. Or if users come across fun events taking place in the DMV, we encourage users to add it to their profile for other Plus One users to view and request an invitation. From there, the event host can accept or decline the invitation. For future improvements, we will give users the opportunity to rate and review the event as well as the attendees. If you have any questions, comments or suggestions, please email them to us at info@plusone.com
